Pizza



Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced green bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a 12 inch by 8 inch pan with foil.
  2. Spread pizza sauce on the bottom of the prepared pan. Sprinkle with cheese, pepperoni, olives, green peppers, onions, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 10 to 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
